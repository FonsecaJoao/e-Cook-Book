e-Cook-Book helps you to keep recipes exactly like you want :)

How to use

  1. Add categorie(s) of recipes which you want to have in your e-Cook-Book, e.g. 'Soups', 'Desserts', 'Dishes from meat'.
  2. Add categorie(s) of ingredients which you want to have in your e-Cook-Book, e.g. 'fruits', 'vegetables', 'herbs', etc.
  3. Add ingredient(s) which you want to use in recipe to chosen categorie of ingredients.
  4. Add needen things which you want to use in recipes.
  5. Add chosen ingredients to recipe.
  6. Add description to recipe.
  7. Add image to recipe.
  8. Add 'Time to make' to recipe.
  9. Add 'No. of portions' to recipe.
  10. Save recipe.

You can have in your personalized e-Cook-Book these categories of recipes and ingredients which are the best for your coooking. This will make your cooking easiest :)

You can also change the color of your e-Cook-Book :) It can be pink, blue or green.
